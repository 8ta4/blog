I made a plugin that helps you edit prose one sentence at a time.

Here's what's included:

- It uses [Groq](https://groq.com) to fetch suggestions quickly.

- It uses highlight groups to display whether sentences pass or fail.

- It displays suggestions in a split window below your current buffer.

You can check out the repo [here](https://github.com/8ta4/word).

It's still rough around the edges. But it's ready for all sentences and purposes. Feel free to share any feedback or questions.
